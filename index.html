Matthew Kim - full stack developer

I arrived at web development after nearly 20 years as a Unix systems administrator at the world's largest, oldest, three-letter, technology company. My programming experience had been limited to scripting. Although it was a minor part of my job the creativity and problem solving made it my favorite part. This led me to software engineering where creativity and problem solving must be applied to both the visual as well as progamming logic. A great thing about writing code/creating software is that it forces you to see that there are very few big wins. You have to find satisfaction along the way. Happy that a function returns what you planned or that a single element appears just as designed. Success is incremental. Just like the rest of life.
